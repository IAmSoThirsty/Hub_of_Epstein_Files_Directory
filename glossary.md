# Glossary

This glossary provides definitions and explanations for terms, organizations, and concepts related to the Epstein files and documentation.

## Terms

### Flight Logs
Records of passengers on private aircraft, particularly relevant to Jeffrey Epstein's aircraft travels. These logs have been used to identify individuals who traveled with or were associated with Epstein.

### Little St. James
A private island in the U.S. Virgin Islands owned by Jeffrey Epstein, often referred to in documents and testimonies. Also known as "Little Saint James Island."

### Deposition
A formal testimony given under oath outside of court, typically as part of the discovery process in legal proceedings.

### Sealed Documents
Legal documents that have been restricted from public access by court order, often containing sensitive information related to ongoing or settled cases.

### Settlement Agreement
A legal agreement between parties to resolve a dispute without going to trial, often including confidentiality clauses.

## Organizations

### Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)
U.S. federal law enforcement agency involved in investigations related to the Epstein case.

### Southern District of New York (SDNY)
Federal judicial district that prosecuted criminal cases related to Jeffrey Epstein.

### Virgin Islands Police Department (VIPD)
Local law enforcement agency with jurisdiction over the U.S. Virgin Islands, including Little St. James.

## Legal Terms

### Non-Prosecution Agreement (NPA)
An agreement between a prosecutor and a defendant where charges are not filed in exchange for certain conditions being met.

### Plea Agreement
A negotiated agreement in a criminal case where the defendant pleads guilty to specific charges in exchange for certain concessions.

### Motion to Unseal
A formal request to a court to make sealed documents available to the public or specific parties.
